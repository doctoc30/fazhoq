Expert FAQ

Do you offer weekend lessons?

Yes! We offer lessons on Saturdays and Sundays at no extra cost.

Absolutely, as long as it is within our coverage zone.

We require 48 hours notice for any lesson cancellations.

The average learner takes 40-45 hours of professional instruction.

Yes, once you have reached a safe standard and have the correct insurance.

Yes, Joe will be your dedicated instructor throughout your journey.
