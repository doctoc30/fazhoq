Common Inquiries

How many lessons do I need to pass?
On average, the DVSA recommends 40 hours of professional tuition. However, our intensive courses can significantly reduce this timeframe.
Do you offer weekend or evening slots?
Yes, we understand our students are busy. We offer flexible scheduling including weekends and late evenings.
Can I pay for lessons individually?
While block booking offers the best value, we do accept individual lesson payments via our online portal.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 48 hours notice for cancellations to ensure our instructors can manage their schedules effectively.
