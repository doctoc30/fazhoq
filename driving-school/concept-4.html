Common Inquiries

What areas of London do you cover?
I cover all South and East London boroughs including Croydon, Bromley, Lewisham, and Greenwich.
Is there a discount for block bookings?
Yes, block bookings of 10 or 20 hours offer a significant discount compared to hourly rates.
Do you offer Automatic lessons?
I specialize in Manual driving tuition to ensure you get a full license for any vehicle type.
What is the "Guaranteed Pass" course?
This is a premium package where you pay once and receive unlimited lessons until you pass.
Can I book lessons on weekends?
Yes, we offer premium weekend slots for busy professionals and students.
How many lessons will I need?
The average beginner needs 30-40 hours, but our intensive courses can cut this down significantly.
Do you help with theory test preparation?
Yes, all block-booking students get a free premium subscription to our theory revision app.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 48 hours notice for any cancellations to ensure our schedule remains fair for all students.
Are you a DVSA approved instructor?
Yes, Joe is a Grade A DVSA Approved Driving Instructor (ADI) with a perfect track record.
Can I use your car for the driving test?
Absolutely. Use of the dual-controlled vehicle for your test is included in all our major packages.
