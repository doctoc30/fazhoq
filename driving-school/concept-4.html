What areas of London do you cover?
I cover all of South London including Croydon, Bromley, Lewisham, Greenwich, and surrounding zones.
How long is each lesson?
Standard lessons are 90 minutes to ensure maximum learning time without fatigue.
Do you offer weekend lessons?
Yes, Saturday slots are available for an additional £5 premium per lesson.
What car will I be learning in?
A modern, dual-controlled 2024 Volkswagen Golf (Manual) or Tesla Model 3 (Automatic).
Can I book an intensive crash course?
Absolutely. Our 1-week and 2-week programs are our most popular high-ticket services.
Do you help with theory test preparation?
Yes, all bundle students get free access to our premium Theory Test Pro app subscription.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 48 hours' notice for cancellations to avoid the full lesson fee being charged.
Are you a fully qualified DVSA instructor?
Yes, Joe is a Grade A DVSA Approved Driving Instructor (ADI) with full CRB/DBS clearance.
Do you offer mock tests?
Yes, I conduct mock tests on actual examiner routes to ensure you are 100% prepared.
Is the test day car hire included?
For our 20-hour and VIP bundles, car hire for the test is included. For others, it is a flat £100 fee.
